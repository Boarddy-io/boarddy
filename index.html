Product Features

Supercharge your daily interactions with clean local memory utilities.

📋

Clipboard Memory

Copy normally. Boarddy remembers. Find copied content from minutes, days, weeks, or months ago. Search it instantly. Never lose important text again.

⚡

Quick Paste

Press Ctrl + Shift + V to search your clipboard history instantly. Find exactly what you need. Paste it immediately. No mouse required. No context switching. No frustration.

🧠

Predictive Key Selection (PKS)

Autocomplete built for speed. Type comm, and Boarddy shows options. Press 2 to instantly insert community. No arrow keys. No mouse. No interruption.

📈

A Keyboard that Learns

Boarddy adapts to how you write. The words and suggestions you use most automatically rise to the top. The longer you use it, the faster it becomes.

📘

Personal Dictionary

Stop fighting autocorrect. Add company names, product names, technical terms, and industry jargon. Boarddy learns your language instead of forcing its own.

📝

Notes Without Friction

Turn temporary information into permanent knowledge. Convert clipboard items into notes instantly. Capture research, ideas, prompts, meeting notes, and references before they disappear.

Your memory belongs to you

Boarddy is local-first. We believe your input history should remain private, secure, and physically stored on your machine.

🔒

Local-First Storage

Your database records, notes, dictionary terms, and settings stay completely on your physical storage drive.

🚫

No Telemetry

We do not record, upload, sell, or track what you copy, type, or search. Zero hidden database transmission.

📡

Offline Native

Boarddy functions perfectly without any active internet connection. No unnecessary cloud dependencies.
